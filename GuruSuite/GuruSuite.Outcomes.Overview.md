# ChangeIS | GuruSuite Business Model Overview

## Vision
**Reclaim time. Deliver outcomes. Build wisdom.**

GuruSuite reimagines consulting as a continuous, intelligent service — combining automation, adaptive reasoning, and human insight to deliver advisory-grade outcomes in days, not months.

---

## 1. Traditional Consulting Model

| Dimension | Legacy Consulting (PwC, KPMG, Deloitte, etc.) |
|------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| **Delivery Model** | Manual, consultant-driven frameworks and workshops |
| **Timeline** | 4–9 months per initiative |
| **Team** | 5–9 consultants + management overhead |
| **Deliverables** | Static documentation, PowerPoint decks |
| **Knowledge Reuse** | Minimal; every engagement restarts from zero |
| **Cost (ITSM Example)** | \$600K – \$1.1M for 8–10 disciplines |
| **Client Experience** | Long, expensive, inconsistent |

---

## 2. GuruSuite Outcome Platform

| Dimension | **GuruSuite Approach** |
|------------|------------------------|
| **Delivery Model** | AI-assisted, outcome-focused engagements powered by the **Insight Engine** *(Didas · Lexa · Sophia)* |
| **Timeline** | 5–10 days per discipline |
| **Team** | 1 advisor + Insight Engine (PIM-guided) |
| **Deliverables** | Dynamic, adaptive frameworks and knowledge libraries |
| **Knowledge Reuse** | Continuous — every project refines the core corpus |
| **Cost (ITSM Example)** | \$10K – \$12K per discipline (≈ \$90K – \$108K total) |
| **Client Experience** | Fast, collaborative, insight-driven |

---

## 3. Engagement Flow

### Phase 1 – Outcome Delivery
- Fixed-scope engagements delivering complete frameworks, templates, and rollout guides.  
- 5–10 days from discovery → delivery.  
- Focused on impact, not effort.

### Phase 2 – Curated Subscription (25 – 33 % of initial cost yearly)
- Annual or semi-annual updates aligned with global standards (ITIL, COBIT, ISO, NIST).  
- “What Changed & Why” briefs explaining every update.  
- Optional review workshops or integration support.  
- Converts one-time outcomes into a living advisory relationship.

## 5. Why It Works

- **PIM ( Person in the Middle ):** Human guidance ensures contextual relevance.  
- **Lexa — The Librarian:** Curates and organizes institutional knowledge.  
- **Didas — The Mentor:** Guides process, context, and tone.  
- **Sophia — The Sage:** Validates reasoning and quality.  

Together they form the **Insight Engine**, the brain of GuruSuite.
